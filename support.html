Frequently asked

Is ReTrace a medical device?

No. ReTrace is a wellbeing support tool, designed to complement, not replace, the care of your clinical team. It does not diagnose, treat or cure any condition.

Does it work offline?

Three of the six exercises (working memory, attention, processing speed) work fully offline. The other three (executive sequencing, everyday memory, visual memory) generate fresh content in your language and need a connection.

Which languages are supported?

17 at launch: English, Welsh, Irish, Spanish (Spain & Mexico), French (France & Canada), German, Italian, Portuguese (Portugal & Brazil), Polish, Japanese, Korean, Hindi, Arabic and Chinese.

Where is my data?

Your settings, sessions and clinician notes stay on your device. Exercise-generation requests are sent to an Ansteyapps server and on to Anthropic's Claude API — they contain only the exercise type, language and difficulty number, nothing personal. Full details on the privacy page.

How do I cancel a subscription?

If you subscribe through the App Store or Google Play, manage and cancel from your account settings on the store. Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled before the renewal date.

Refunds

Refunds are processed by the store where you bought the app. For Apple, use Report a Problem (reportaproblem.apple.com). For Google Play, see support.google.com/googleplay. If a store decides against a refund and you believe there's a defect with the app, write to us.
