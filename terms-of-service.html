1. What ReTrace is

ReTrace is a multilingual cognitive rehabilitation support app designed for adults recovering from stroke, brain injury and other neurological conditions. It offers six exercise domains — working memory, attention, processing speed, executive function, everyday memory and visual memory — across ten adaptive difficulty levels each, in seventeen languages. Three of the six domains run entirely offline; the other three generate fresh content in your chosen language using a server proxy to Anthropic's Claude API. See the Privacy Policy for full details of how this works.

8. Privacy and data

ReTrace stores your settings, exercise history and clinician notes on your device. For three of the six exercise domains the app sends a minimal, non-identifying request (exercise type, language and difficulty) to our server, which proxies it to Anthropic. No personal data, scores, answers or identifiers are transmitted. The Anthropic API key is held server-side and is never accessible to the app.

Full details are in our Privacy Policy.
