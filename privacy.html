Privacy policy

Last updated 29 June 2026 · Ansteyapps Limited, England & Wales (company number 16291443)

ReTrace is a cognitive rehabilitation app from Ansteyapps Limited. We take privacy seriously: ReTrace is designed to keep your practice on your device wherever practical, and to send only what is needed for the app to work.

What we collect

ReTrace processes the following data:

  • On-device only. Your settings (language, accessibility preferences), your exercise history (sessions, minutes, scores, difficulty levels) and any clinician notes you type in the app are stored on your device. They never leave it unless you choose to export or print a report.
  • Sent to our server. When you start an exercise that uses generated content (executive sequencing, everyday memory, visual memory), the app sends only a short request describing the exercise type, the target language and the difficulty level. No personal data, account information or your previous answers are sent. The server passes this to Anthropic's Claude API and returns the generated exercise.
  • Account and billing. If you create an account or subscribe, your email address and payment processor reference are stored by our subscription provider. We do not see or store your card details.

What we do not collect

  • We do not sell, rent or share your data with advertisers.
  • We do not include third-party trackers, advertising SDKs or behavioural analytics in the app.
  • We do not collect health data, diagnoses, identifying medical information or anything you have not chosen to put into the clinician notes field.

Anthropic and exercise generation

Exercises 4–6 (executive sequencing, everyday memory, visual memory) are generated in your language at runtime using Anthropic's Claude. Requests are proxied through an Ansteyapps server that holds the API key. The request contains only the type of exercise, the language and the difficulty number — for example "generate one 'remember the message' exercise in Welsh at level 3". No personal data is included. Anthropic's data handling is described at anthropic.com/legal/privacy; under Anthropic's commercial terms, API inputs and outputs are not used for model training.

Children

ReTrace is intended for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Your rights (UK GDPR)

You have the right to access, correct, delete or restrict the processing of your personal data, and to object to processing or request portability. Because ReTrace stores most data on your device, deletion is usually as simple as removing the app or clearing its data. For account-related requests, email privacy@ansteyapps.com.

Legal basis

We process the minimal data described above on the basis of your consent (when you create an account) and our legitimate interest in providing and improving the app (for the exercise-generation request).

Data retention

Account data is kept while your account is active and deleted within 30 days of account closure. The exercise-generation request and response are not logged with any identifier and are not retained beyond the time needed to fulfil the request.

Security

API keys are held server-side. Communication between the app and our server is encrypted in transit (HTTPS). On-device data is stored within the app's sandbox.

Changes

If we change this policy we will update the date above and, for material changes, notify you in the app.
